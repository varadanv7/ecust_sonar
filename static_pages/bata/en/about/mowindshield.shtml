Vehicles with Metal Oxide Windshields
The vehicles listed below are equipped with special windshields that contain metal in the glass that prevents the FasTrak toll tag from being read properly. Customers with these windshields must obtain exterior toll tags. There may be other vehicles that have specialized windshields as an option that may prevent a FasTrak toll tag from being read properly. If you are unsure about whether your vehicle has one of these windshields, contact the dealer where you purchased your car.
