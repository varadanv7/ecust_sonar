Messages
If you have a Bay Area FasTrak or License Plate account and opt in to receive Mobile Alerts, you will receive notifications on your mobile phone of changes to your account, including account replenishment, credit card expiration and low balance notifications. The message frequency will vary based on your account activity during a given month.
How to Opt In
To opt in or subscribe to Mobile Alerts, you must have a Bay Area FasTrak or License Plate account. Account holders may login at www.bayareafastrak.org, select Account Settings, confirm that a valid mobile phone number has been provided, read the Terms and Conditions, then select "Yes, I'd like to receive text messages regarding changes to my account.", and click Update.
How to Opt Out
You may opt out of this Service at any time by texting STOP, END, QUIT, CANCEL, or UNSUBSCRIBE to TOLLS (86557) or by updating your Account Settings online and unselect "Yes, I'd like to receive text messages regarding changes to my account.", and click Update.
How to Get Help or Support
To get help, text HELP to TOLLS (86557) or for customer service call 1-877-BAY-TOLL (1-877-229-8655)
