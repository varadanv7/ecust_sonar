FasTrak Flex. The carpool tag for Bay Area Express Lanes.

FasTrak Flex is required for carpools to travel toll-free in the new I-580 Express Lanes, opening early next year. FasTrak Flex can also be used in all Bay Area Express Lanes for solo or carpool travel.
