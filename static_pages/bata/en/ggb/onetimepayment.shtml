ONE-TIME PAYMENT

Paying your Golden Gate Bridge toll is easy using a one-time payment. You can pay before or within 48 hours of your crossing. If you do not make a payment within 48 hours of your crossing, an invoice will be sent to the address of the vehicle's registered owner.

To pay for a Golden Gate Bridge toll online, you will need a credit card, the vehicle license plate and state of registration, and the dates of your toll crossings.
