Open a License Plate Account

You will need the following information for the enrollment process. For security reasons, you will have 15 minutes to complete the sign up process before your session times out.

What you'll need

Valid Email Address
Credit Card
License Plate Information
A valid e-mail address is required in order to enroll in a License Plate account. A valid credit card is required to register online. If you want to open an account using cash or a check, you'll need to contact Customer Service for assistance You must have your license plate number and state for each vehicle you would like to register.
