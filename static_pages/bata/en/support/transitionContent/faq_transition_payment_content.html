My invoice is due during the system upgrade. I need to make a payment right now. What do I do?

Making payments through the postal mail is still possible. You can mail a check to the address listed on your invoice. Do not send cash.

If the due date on your invoice falls on October 31st through November 3rd, an extension through Friday, November 7th will be provided for you to make a payment.

My violation notice is due during the system upgrade. I need to make a payment right now. What do I do?

Making payments through the postal mail is still possible. You can mail a check to the address listed on your invoice. Do not send cash.

If the due date on your notice of violation falls on October 31st through November 3rd, you will be provided an extension through Friday, November 7th to make a payment.

If you choose to sign up for a FasTrak account, we'll waive the violation fees! After the system upgrade has been completed, start the new account enrollment process and backdate the start date of your vehicle to include the date of your toll crossing. You'll only be responsible for the tolls you owe.

Will the cash payment network locations still be able to accept payments during the system upgrade?

No. The cash payment network locations will be unable to accept payments until 8:30am Tuesday, November 4th.
