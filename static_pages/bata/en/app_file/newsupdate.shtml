Golden Gate Bridge Toll Increases 25 Cents Starting July 1, 2015
On July 1, 2015 the Golden Gate Bridge toll will increase by 25 cents for FasTrak from $6.00 to $6.25 and for Pay-by-Plate from $7.00 to $7.25. This toll increase is part of a five-year, incremental toll increase adopted by the Golden Gate Bridge, Highway and Transportation District's Board of Directors in February 2014.

This toll increase will raise $138 million over the five years which will assist greatly in reducing the District's $142 million 5-year shortfall.

FasTrak Customer Service Center Closed for the Fourth of July holiday

Friday, July 3rd and Saturday, July 4th.

The Customer Service Center will be closed for the Independence Day holiday weekend: Friday, July 3rd and Saturday, July 4th. Customer Service Representatives will not be available by phone. The walk-in center, located at 62 First Street in San Francisco, will also be closed. Account access and payments are available online and by using the automated phone system. The Customer Service Center will re-open at its normal time, 8:30 am, on Monday, July 6th.

FasTrak wishes you a safe and happy Independence Day!
