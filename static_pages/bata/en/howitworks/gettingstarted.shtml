HOW IT WORKS

Open a FasTrak account today! Follow the instructions below to get started with FasTrak.

Open an Account

Mount It

Mount the tag to the inside of your vehicle's windshield. Proper placement is key to ensuring your tag is recognized in the FasTrak lane.

Learn how to mount your tag

Use It

You can use your toll tag anywhere you see the FasTrak logo. You can use your tag on all eight Bay Area bridges without having to stop at the toll plaza, take advantage of the express lanes in Northern and Southern California and skip the line to the parking pay booth at the San Francisco International Airport.
