THREE EASY STEPS TO KEEP MOVING WITH FASTRAK
STEP 1. Open a FasTrak Account
Starting FasTrak is easy. When you open a new account, you'll order one or more FasTrak toll tags to be sent to you. Or, you can pick up a toll tag at a retail store, register it, and create an account. On the Sign Up page, you can also find a retail store to pick up a toll tag for immediate use.

Sign Up
STEP 2. Mount your FasTrak toll tag

Follow the mounting instructions that come with the toll tag so that it is detected and works properly. Some windshields may require special mounting.

How To Mount a Toll Tag
STEP 3. Use FasTrak
Pay tolls electronically at all eight Bay Area toll bridges, express lanes, and garages displaying the FasTrak logo. Receive a $1 discount on the Golden Gate Bridge.

Where To Use
Get Carpool Discounts or Travel Toll-Free
Pay less or travel toll-free as a carpool, motorcycle, or eligible Clean Air Vehicle (CAV) with a FasTrak toll tag.
