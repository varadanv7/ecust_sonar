Purchasing a SunPass is easy as there are over 3,100 retail locations throughout Florida.


Download and print an application: Private or Fleet


SunPass can also be purchased at all Turnpike service plazas, Turnpike gas stations, SunPass Service Centers, select Florida Welcome Centers, Visitor Centers, Rest Areas, and select County Tax Collector’s Offices.
