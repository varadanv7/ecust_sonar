Warranty Information

The SunPass Portable transponder carries a two-year warranty (excluding suction cups) from the date of purchase.
The SunPass external mount transponder carries a two-year warranty from the date of purchase.
The SunPass Mini Sticker transponder has a 45 day manufacturer's warranty for defects and malfunctions from date of purchase.

Should your transponder malfunction, please visit www.sunpass.com (automatic navigation login-> transponder listing) to check your transponder status. If defective status is an available status change option, a replacement transponder will be provided. You must return the defective transponder to avoid a replacement charge. If defective status is not shown, you must purchase a new transponder.
