Open a New FasTrak Account
You will need the following information for the enrollment process. For security reasons, you will have 30 minutes to complete the sign up process before your session times out.


What you'll need
Valid Email Address
A valid e-mail address is required to enroll in a FasTrak account online.
Credit Card
A valid credit card is required to register online. If you want to open an account using cash or a check, you'll need to contact Customer Service for assistance.
License Plate Information
You must know the make, model, year, license plate#, state of registration, and start date for each vehicle you want to register to your account and the number of tags you want to order.

If you already have a toll tag that you picked up at a retail store, you need to start here to open a new account.
