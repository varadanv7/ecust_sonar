How to Enter Your License Plate Number

Regular License Plates

Enter the letters and numbers that appear on the license plate. There will be up to 7 letters and numbers.

Plates That Start With Vertical Letters

Organizational license plates often have letters arranged vertically in front of the number. These vertical letters are part of the license plate number. If you have a plate with letters in front, enter the vertical part of the plate number first, then the rest of the number.
