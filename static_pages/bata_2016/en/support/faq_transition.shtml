FasTrak System Update FAQs

FasTrak customer information and payment services are paused during our system upgrade. The Customer Service Center, including the in-person counter and the live customer service call center, is also closed. Services will be available again starting Tuesday, November 4th at 8:30am.

All FasTrak toll facilities are up and running during the system upgrade. Please continue to use FasTrak toll bridges, express lanes and the SFO parking garages as you normally would.
