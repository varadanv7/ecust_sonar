My invoice payment was due during the system upgrade, but the system was unavailable to receive my payment. Will a grace period be provided?

If the due date on your invoice is October 31st,, November 1st, November 2nd, or November 3rd, you have through Friday, November 7th to make a payment.

My violation notice payment was due during the system upgrade, but the system was unavailable to receive my payment. Will a grace period be provided?

If the due date on your notice of violation is October 31st,, November 1st, November 2nd, or November 3rd, you have through Friday, November 7th to make a payment.

If you choose to sign up for a FasTrak account, we’ll waive the violation fees! Start the new account enrollment process and backdate the start date of your vehicle to include the date of your toll crossing. You’ll only be responsible for the tolls you owe.
