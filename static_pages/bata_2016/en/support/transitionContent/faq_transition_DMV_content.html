I received a letter regarding unpaid tolls from a collection agency. I need customer service assistance.

If you have a dispute or question about a letter you received from Law Enforcement Systems regarding outstanding violations, the FasTrak Customer Service Center is not able to assist you. Since your case was turned over to the collection agency, you will need to contact them directly to discuss your case further.

I'm trying to register my vehicle and have a DMV hold due to unpaid tolls. I need customer service assistance.

If there is a DMV hold on your vehicle registration, DMV can take your payment to release the hold. You do not need to wait for the Customer Service Center to re-open to be able to resolve this issue. Any disputes over amounts due can be resolved once the system upgrade is completed and a refund can be sent to you if it is warranted.
