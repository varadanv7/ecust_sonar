We're working hard to make driving in the Bay Area more enjoyable! Thank you for your patience as we made improvements to our system.

All account and payment functions are now available via the website, the telephone system, the cash payment network locations, and our walk-in customer service center.

Invoices and Violations
If the due date of your invoice or notice of violation is October 31st, November 1st, November 2nd, or November 3rd, you have through Friday, November 7th to make your payment without further penalty.

Golden Gate Bridge users
If you crossed the Golden Gate Bridge during the system upgrade and are not a FasTrak or License Plate account holder, an invoice (or toll bill) for the toll amount only will be mailed to the vehicle’s registered owner. You can just wait for your invoice in the mail and follow the instruction on the notice to pay your tolls.

Alternately, if you want to set up a One-Time payment to pay for tolls that occurred on October 31st, November 1st, November 2nd, or November 3rd, you have through Friday, November 7th to set up a One-Time payment (OTP). Remember to backdate the vehicle start date to include the date of the first crossing.
